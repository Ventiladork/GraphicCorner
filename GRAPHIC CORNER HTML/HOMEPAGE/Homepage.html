WHAT IS GRAPHIC DESIGN?

Let us first begin defining what graphic design is. The term “graphic design” was too narrow wherein it defines the craft that most of the professionals use to create a visual content. It can also be defined as a variety of disciplines of design including digital and web design. But traditional graphic design is print design like posters, book covers, magazine layout, advertisements, package design, as well as logo and branding.

In essence, as a graphic designer, you could combine visual content such as illustrations or photography with typography to deliver a message. Communication is vital. But where did graphic design begin, and how has it transitioned over time?
